## 3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data under the following legal bases:

### 3.1 Contract Performance (Article 6(1)(b) UK GDPR)

Processing necessary to:
- Create and manage your account
- Provide contract creation and management services
- Process payments and escrow transactions
- Deliver LITL referral services

### 3.2 Legal Obligation (Article 6(1)(c) UK GDPR)

Processing required to:
- Comply with anti-money laundering regulations
- Respond to lawful requests from authorities
- Maintain financial records for tax purposes
- Meet data protection requirements

### 3.3 Legitimate Interests (Article 6(1)(f) UK GDPR)

Processing necessary for our legitimate interests, including:
- Fraud prevention and security
- Service improvement and analytics
- Marketing to existing customers
- Enforcing our terms of service

**Your Right to Object:** You can object to processing based on legitimate interests by contacting us at privacy@allsquared.co.uk.

### 3.4 Consent (Article 6(1)(a) UK GDPR)

Where we rely on consent, including:
- Marketing communications (non-customers)
- Non-essential cookies
- Sharing data with marketing partners

**Your Right to Withdraw:** You can withdraw consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

## 8. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the following rights:

### 8.1 Right of Access (Article 15)

You can request a copy of your personal data. We will respond within one month.

### 8.2 Right to Rectification (Article 16)

You can request correction of inaccurate personal data.

### 8.3 Right to Erasure (Article 17)

You can request deletion of your data where:
- Data is no longer necessary
- You withdraw consent
- You object to processing
- Data was unlawfully processed

**Exceptions:** We may retain data required for legal compliance or defence of claims.

### 8.4 Right to Restriction (Article 18)

You can request restriction of processing while:
- Accuracy is verified
- Objection is considered
- Legal claims are pending

### 8.5 Right to Data Portability (Article 20)

You can request your data in a structured, commonly used format for transfer to another provider.

### 8.6 Right to Object (Article 21)

You can object to processing based on legitimate interests. We will stop unless we demonstrate compelling legitimate grounds.

### 8.7 Rights Related to Automated Decision-Making (Article 22)

You have the right to:
- Not be subject to solely automated decisions with significant effects
- Request human review of automated decisions
- Express your point of view

### 8.8 Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any right:
- Email: privacy@allsquared.co.uk
- Use the "Privacy Settings" in your account
- Write to our registered address

We will respond within one month. Complex requests may take up to three months with notice.
