### FAQ Section

**Q: Is AllSquared a law firm?**
> No. AllSquared is a technology platform that provides contract templates and facilitates transactions. We don't provide legal advice. For legal questions, we offer connections to SRA-regulated solicitors through our Lawyer-in-the-Loop service.

**Q: How is my money protected?**
> All escrow funds are held by Transpact Ltd, an FCA-authorised escrow provider (Reference: 546279). Your money is held in segregated client accounts in accordance with FCA regulations.

**Q: What happens if there's a dispute?**
> First, our AI-assisted mediation tool helps both parties find common ground. If you can't agree, you can escalate to a qualified human mediator. We never release disputed funds until there's an agreement or resolution.

**Q: Can I use AllSquared for any type of work?**
> AllSquared is designed for service-based work including freelance projects, home improvement, events, and professional services. We have templates for many industries and can generate custom contracts for unique situations.

**Q: What fees do I pay?**
> AllSquared charges a small transaction fee on escrow payments (2.5% on Free, 2.0% on Starter, 1.5% on Pro, 1.0% on Enterprise). This includes payment processing and escrow protection. Paid plans have monthly subscription fees with lower transaction rates.
