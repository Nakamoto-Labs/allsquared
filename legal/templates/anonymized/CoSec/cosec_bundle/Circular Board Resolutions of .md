[PARTY] Resources Limited
ACN [PHONE](the Company)

Record of written resolutions of the directors of the Company in accordance with clause 16.11 of the constitution of the Company

We, the undersigned, being all the directors of the Company, certify that we are in favour of each of the resolutions set out below.

1.  Background

    1.  It is noted that the Company intends to appoint Lau Wai Ming as non-executive director of the Company.

    2.  It is noted that the Company has received a consent to act as director from Lau Wai Ming.

    3.  To facilitate the appointment of Lau Wai Ming as non-executive director of the Company, the Company intends to enter into the following documents with Lau Wai Ming:

        a.  a non-executive director appointment letter; and

        b.  a deed of access, indemnity and insurance,

            (together, the Lau Documents).

    4.  The Company also intends to enter into a non-executive director appointment letter with Yongji Duan to reduce the terms of his previous appointment as director of the Company to writing (Duan Document).

    5.  In these resolutions, the Lau Documents and the Duan Document are together referred to as the Documents.

    6.  It is noted that execution versions of the Documents were circulated with these resolutions.

2.  Effect of the Documents

    1.  Having regard to all the relevant circumstances and the terms of the Documents, it is determined that:

        a.  the execution, delivery and performance of the Company’s obligations under the Documents and the transactions evidenced by the Documents are in the Company’s best interests and for the commercial benefit of the Company and will not adversely affect the financial position of the Company; and

        b.  the entry into and performance of the Documents by the Company and effecting the transactions under them will not contravene, violate or cause a breach or default under:

            i.  the Corporations Act 2001 (Cth) or any other applicable law;

            ii. any other statute by which the Company is bound or to which any of its property is subject, including any applicable replaceable rules in the Corporations Act 2001 (Cth) and the Company’s constitution; or

            iii. any document or transaction to which the Company is bound or to which any of its property is subject.

3.  Resolutions

Appointment of Lau Wai Ming as director

1.  The directors resolved that:

    a.  the consent to act as director from Lau Wai Ming be accepted by the Company; and

    b.  the Company appoint Lau Wai Ming as director of the Company effective from [DATE].

Entry into transaction and performance of obligations under the Documents

2.  The directors considered whether it is deemed desirable and in the best interests of the Company to approve the entry into the transactions contemplated by, and the performance of the obligations of the Company under, the Documents (in such final form and substance and among such parties as the director or directors executing the document thinks fit), and resolved that entry into that transaction and performance of those obligations are approved.

Entry into the Documents

3.  The directors considered whether it is deemed desirable and in the best interests of the Company to approve the entry by the Company into the Documents, and resolved that the form, terms and provisions of the Documents (in such final form and substance and among such parties as the director or directors executing the documents thinks fit) be approved, and that the Company should enter into the Documents and the transactions contemplated by the Documents.

Execution of documents

4.  The directors considered whether it is deemed desirable and in the best interests of the Company to authorise any director and the company secretary of the Company under section 127 of the Corporations Act 2001 (Cth) to execute the Documents on behalf of the Company, and resolved that any two directors or one director and the company secretary of the Company be authorised to execute the Documents on behalf of the Company and for the executed Documents to be delivered to the relevant counterparty.

Authorised Officers

5.  The directors resolved that each director and the company secretary be appointed severally as signatories of the Company and be authorised severally to:

    a.  do everything that is required to be, or capable of being, done by the Company (including, but not limited to, certification, execution and delivery of documents and notices) in accordance with, or incidental to, the above resolutions or under the Documents;

    b.  complete the necessary lodgements with the Australian Securities and Investments Commission in connection with the above resolutions; and

    c.  take any other action necessary or, in the opinion of the person undertaking the act, desirable in connection with the Documents or any of the above resolutions.

Minutes

6.  The directors resolved that the passage of these resolutions be recorded in the minute book of the Company as required by section 251A of the Corporations Act.

Ratification

7.  The directors resolved that any action taken by the Company before the date of these resolutions that would have been authorised by the resolutions set out in this document had it been taken after the date of these resolutions be ratified and approved.

4.  Interested Directors

    1.  It is noted that, in accordance with sections 191 and 192 of the Corporations Act 2001 (Cth) and in accordance with the Company’s constitution, Yongji Duan properly disclosed to each other director that he has a personal interest in the Company entering into the Duan Document, and each other director acknowledged such interest.

    2.  It is noted that, in light of his personal interest, Yongji Duan abstained from voting on these resolutions to the extent they related to the approval for the Company to enter into and perform the Duan Document.
