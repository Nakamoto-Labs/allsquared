Token Warrant Agreement

  This side letter (Agreement) confirms that in connection with the execution of the SAFE between [Insert] (Company) and the party described as the ‘Purchaser’ in the SAFE (Investor or you), the Investor may be entitled to purchase Tokens pursuant to the terms and conditions in this Agreement.

Definitions

  Agreement means this side letter between the Company and the Investor.

  Application means the software application developed by the Company, referred to as ‘Block Earner’ or such other name as determined by the Company.

  Business Day means a day (other than a Saturday, Sunday or public holiday) on which commercial banks are open for business in Sydney;

  Corporations Act means the Corporations Act 2001 (Cth).

  Investor Tokens means all Tokens retained by the Company or Token Issuer (whichever is relevant) following the Token Launch retained for investors, but excluding:

(a) Tokens allocated to third party contributors and strategic partners;

(b) Tokens allocated for future distribution to advisors, employees and other service providers as compensation or as consideration for value; and

(c) Tokens otherwise allocated primarily for the purpose of capital expenditures or operational expenses of the Company, as determined by the board of directors of the Company in its sole discretion.

  Pro Rata Portion means the percentage amount equivalent to the SAFE Purchase Amount divided by the SAFE Valuation Cap.

  SAFE means the simple agreement for future equity agreement entered into between the Company and Investor on or about the date of this Agreement.

  Token and Tokens are, in each case, with respect to the Application:

(a) cryptocurrencies, decentralised application tokens, protocol tokens, cryptographic digital tokens or coins, virtual currencies, blockchain-based assets or other similar digital assets;

(b) instruments exchangeable, exercisable or convertible into the items in paragraph (a); or

(c) instruments, documents, agreements, understandings, contracts or arrangements that provide for the right to receive the items in paragraph (a)

  Token Issuer means any corporate entity (including a company, trust, association, partnership, foundation), enterprise, digital collective governing structure (including decentralised autonomous organisations) or other organisation that:

(a) is not the Company;

(b) is the issuer of the Tokens in the Token Launch

(c) in the case of a corporate entity, either:

    i.  controls, is controlled by, or is under common control with the Company, or any of its parents or subsidiaries; or

    ii. was formed, incorporated or founded by the Company or any of its parents or subsidiaries; or

    iii. has a services contract, agreement, relationship, understanding or arrangement with the Company or any of its parents or subsidiaries, and

  is materially related or connected to the Company or its business with respect to the Application as conducted or as proposed to be conducted.

  Token Launch means the bona fide transaction or series of transactions, pursuant to which the Company or Token Issuer whichever is relevant) issues Tokens of the Application.

  Token Offering means the sale, grant, creation, issuance, sponsorship or distribution of any Tokens by the Company, a Token Issuer, or on the Company’s or such Token Issuer’s behalf in exchange for a delivery of purchase price, a contribution, or any other items of value, including other Tokens. The grant or distribution of some Tokens for no consideration will not disqualify a Token Offering from being considered as such. For the avoidance of doubt, a proposed sale, grant, creation, issuance, sponsorship or distribution of any Tokens by the Company or Token Issuer on behalf of a third party, including a customer of the Company in the ordinary course of business, shall not constitute a Token Offering.

  Token Sale Agreement means the sale agreement under which the Company or Token Issuer (whichever is relevant) will issue Tokens in respect of the Application.

  Token Restriction means the prohibitions set by the Company or Token Issuer (whichever is relevant) on the Investor’s ability to sell, transfer, spend, exchange or otherwise deal with the Tokens on the Application until such Tokens are vested under this Agreement.

  Vesting Period means a period as determined by the Company or Token Issuer, beginning on the date which the Allocated Tokens are issued to the Investor under clause 2.3(c).

Conditional right to purchase Tokens

Token Launch

  The Company or a Token Issuer may (but is not obliged to) perform a Token Launch at a future date in its sole discretion.

Investor’s conditional right

(a) Subject to a Token Launch occurring and applicable law, the Investor has a conditional right to purchase up to its Pro Rata Portion of the Investor Tokens on the same terms and the same price as other eligible participants purchasing Investor Tokens in the initial Token Offering or another discounted price (as determined by the Company or Token Issuer).

  For example: if there are 100 Investor Tokens after the Token Launch and the Investor has a Pro Rata Portion of 10%, the Investor will be eligible to purchase up to 10 Investor Tokens.

(b) When the Investor’s right in clause 2.2(a) arises, the Company or Token Issuer will provide the Investor with a written notice (Purchase Notice) specifying:

    i.  the terms of issue of the Investor Tokens (including a copy of any relevant Token Sale Agreement if applicable);

    ii. the total number of Investor Tokens available for purchase by the Investor up to its Pro Rata Portion; and

    iii. any Token Restrictions that are applicable to the Investor Tokens.

(c) The Investor must notify the Company or Token Issuer (whichever is relevant) within 10 Business Days (Exercise Period) of receiving the Purchase Notice of its election to irrevocably exercise its right under clause 2.2(a) and the number of Investor Tokens (up to its Pro Rata Portion) the Investor wishes to purchase (Allocated Tokens).

(d) The Investor acknowledges and accepts that if it fails to notify the Company or Token Issuer whichever is relevant) of its election during the Exercise Period, neither the Company nor Token Issuer is required to issue any Investor Tokens to the Investor and the Investor’s right to purchase Investor Tokens expires.

Completion

(a) If the Investor notifies the Company of its election to exercise its right under clause 2.2(a), the Company or Token Issuer (whichever is relevant) will give the Investor a notice setting out its Allocated Tokens and the total purchase amount.

(b) Within 5 Business Days of receiving a notice under clause 2.3(a), the Investor must provide the Company or Token Issuer (whichever is relevant) with:

    i.  an executed copy of the Token Sale Agreement (if applicable);

    ii. the purchase amount; and

    iii. the Investor’s digital wallet address details to issue the Allocated Tokens.

(c) Within 5 Business Days of receiving the items in clause 2.3(b), the Company or Token Issuer whichever is relevant) will issue the Allocated Tokens to the Investor’s digital wallet address.

(d) For clarity, neither the Company nor the Token Issuer are liable for:

    i.  any delayed or failed Token issuance due to congestion on any cryptocurrency network or any other circumstances beyond their reasonable control;

    ii. the Investor nominating a digital wallet address that does not support the Token type; or

    iii. any loss associated with the Investor providing incorrect digital wallet address details

1.  Conditions

Token purchase subject to conditions

(a) The issuance of any Tokens under this Agreement is subject to a Token Launch occurring. The Investor acknowledges and agrees that it has no rights against any entity (including the Company or Token Issuer) if there is no Token Launch.

(b) Any Tokens issued to the Investor under this Agreement will be subject to:

    i.  the Token Sale Agreement (if applicable), including all requirements, disclaimers, representations, warranties and limitations included therein;

    ii. any reasonable requirements, conditions or restrictions imposed by the Company or Token Issuer whichever is relevant) in connection with the issuance of Tokens on the Application generally. Such requirements, conditions or restrictions will be disclosed to the Investor prior to issuing the Purchase Notice; and

    iii. the Token Restrictions in accordance with clause 3.2.

(a) For the avoidance of doubt, the Investor acknowledges and agrees that:

    i.  neither Tokens nor this Agreement is considered a financial product as defined in Chapter 7 of the Corporations Act; and

    ii. no entity is required to issue Tokens to the Investor, where doing so would give rise to disclosure requirements under Chapter 6D or Chapter 7 of the Corporations Act.

Token Restrictions

(a) All Tokens (including Allocated Tokens) issued to the Investor under this Agreement will be subject to the Token Restrictions.

(b) The Tokens issued to the Investor shall vest and cease to be subject to the Token Restrictions in equal portions at the end of each quarter during the Vesting Period. For clarity, all Tokens issued under this Agreement shall be fully vested and cease to be subject to the Token Restrictions at the end of the Vesting Period.

No rights additional rights attached to Tokens

(a) Unless explicitly stated in a Token Sale Agreement or any other agreement relating to the issuance of Tokens, the Investor expressly acknowledges that you are aware that a Token does not provide you with any rights, including:

    i.  any ownership right, interest, share (or equivalent right) in the Company, Token Issuer, or any other company;

    ii. any right or future right to receive an interest in any intellectual property rights relating to the Company, any other company or the Tokens;

    iii. any right to demand that the Company, Token Issuer or any of its related bodies corporate purchase or otherwise acquire Tokens from you;

    iv. any other form of participation in or relating to the Company or Token Issuer; or

    v.  other right that may be implied by law.

General

Definitions and interpretation

Unless otherwise defined in this Agreement, all capitalised terms shall have the meanings given in the SAFE.

Variation

  The terms of this Agreement may only be amended, waived or modified with the written consent of the Company and the Investor.

Notices

(a) All notices in connection with this Agreement must be:

    i.  in legible writing and in English; and

    ii. addressed, marked to the attention of the person specified and sent to the receiving party at the address or email set out in this Agreement or otherwise as notified by that party from time to time.

(b) A notice will be considered to have been received:

    i.  if sent to the address, three Business Days after posting (or seven Business Days after posting if sent from one country to another); or

    ii. by email, four hours after the time sent (as recorded on the device from which the sender sent the email), unless the sender receives an email in response specifying that the email did not reach the intended receiving party or the receiving party is out of the office,

  but if a notice is delivered to the address, or is received by the receiving party’s email, on a day that is not a Business Day, or after 5:00pm (local time) on a Business Day, the notice will be considered to have been received by the receiving party at 9.00 am on the next Business Day.

  This Agreement may be executed in one or more counterparts, each of which will be deemed an original, but all of which together will constitute one and the same agreement.
