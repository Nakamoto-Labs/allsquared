  Author introductory note

  This precedent is an application for shares.

  Before shares can be allotted to a shareholder, an application for shares must be made. The shareholder must agree to accept the allotted shares, and to be bound by the rules of the constitution of the company. The shareholder must also permit his or her name to be placed on the register of member of the company.
  ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  Application for shares

  To the directors of [company name] (ABN [ABN]) (Company)

(a) I, [proposed shareholder name], of [address], apply for [number] [class of shares] shares at $[amount] per share (Allotted Shares).

(b) I enclose $[amount].

(c) I agree to accept the Allotted Shares, to be bound by the rules of the constitution of the Company and authorise my name to be placed on the register of members in respect of the Allotted Shares.
