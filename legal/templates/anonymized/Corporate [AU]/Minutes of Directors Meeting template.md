--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  This precedent is a template which can be used for the minutes of a directors’ meeting.                                                                                                                 
  --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- --- ------------------------------------------------------------------
  Changes to a company structure must be done by a company resolution and a lodgement of ASIC form 484. These structure changes include changes to:                                                       

                                                                                                                                                                                                      •   Registered Office: [ADDRESS]

                                                                                                                                                                                                      •   officeholder (officer, director or secretary);

                                                                                                                                                                                                      •   proprietary company member;

                                                                                                                                                                                                      •   name of officeholder or member;

                                                                                                                                                                                                      •   ultimate holding company;

                                                                                                                                                                                                      •   appointment or cessation of officeholder;

                                                                                                                                                                                                      •   cancellation of shares;

                                                                                                                                                                                                      •   issue of shares;

                                                                                                                                                                                                      •   share structure; and

                                                                                                                                                                                                      •   register of members for proprietary companies.

  This precedent can be used to record the minutes of the directors’ meeting, at which these resolutions will be made.                                                                                    

  The minutes of the director’s meeting in this precedent include a number of variables, depending on what changes are being made to the company details. The irrelevant details should be deleted.       
  --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  Minutes of a meeting of the directors of [Company name] (ACN [ACN]) (Company)

  1  Meeting information

  Held at: [address]

  Date: [date]

  Time: [time]

  Present:

(a) [names of directors present].

  2  Election of chairperson

  [Name of director] was elected as chairperson of the meeting.

  3  Minutes of previous meeting

  The minutes of previous meeting of directors were read out and confirmed.

  4  [ Optional: Share transfer

(a) It is noted that the Company received an executed share transfer form in respect of [number] fully paid [class of shares] shares (Shares) from [name] of [address] to [name] of [address].

(b) It was resolved that that the Company:

(i) approve the transfer of the Shares;

1.  amend the registers and journals of the Company to reflect the transfer of Shares;

1.  issue new certificate in respect of the Shares;

1.  cancel the existing share certificate for the Shares; and

1.  make all necessary notifications required by law in relation to the transfer of Shares including the filing of a form 484 with ASIC.]

  5  [ Optional: Resignation of a director]

  It was resolved that the resignation of [name of resigning director] be accepted.]

  6  [ Optional: Approval of sale agreement

  It was resolved that:

(a) the company is authorised to enter into the [details of sale agreement] (Sale Agreement);

(b) the Sale Agreement is approved; and

(c) the board is authorised to do all things necessary, at the board’s full discretion, to give full force and effect to the present resolution, including the signing of documents and any other acts.]

  7  [ Optional: Appointment of a director

  It was resolved that, having consented to act as the director of the company, [name of new director] be appointed as a director of the Company.]

  8  [ Optional: Change of registered place of business]

  It was resolved that the Company’s Registered Office: [ADDRESS]

  9  Closure

  There being no further business to discuss, the meeting was closed.
