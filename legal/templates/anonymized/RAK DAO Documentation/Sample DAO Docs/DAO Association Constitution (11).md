CONSTITUTION

1.  OF

    Solarakis DAO Limited

    A DAO ASSOCIATION

1.  FORMATION

    The members have formed a Solarakis DAO Limited ("DAO Association") subject to RAK DAO Association Regulations 2024 (“Regulations”) of the Ras Al Khaimah Digital Assets Oasis Free Zone located in the Emirate of Ras Al Khaimah, United Arab Emirates.

    This Constitution is intended to provide for the regulation and management of the affairs of the DAO Association. Except for the provisions of the Regulations that specifically may not be modified by the decision of the members, to the extent of any contradiction between the provisions of this Constitution and the Regulations, the provisions of the Regulations shall govern and control, and each member hereby consents to such contradiction or variation.

    This Constitution can be in the form of a Smart Contract.

2.  Purpose

    The purpose of the DAO Association is specified in Article 3 of the Memorandum of Association of the DAO Association (the "DAO Association Memorandum") and is as follows.

    Education DAOs

3.  NON-PROFIT NATURE

    The DAO Association may generate returns or profits, provided that they are reinvested or reallocated towards an activity that aligns with the DAO Association’s purpose. The distribution of assets, income or profits to the Members, including Council and Founding Members, officers, employees or other individuals, is prohibited.

18. REGISTRATION

    The DAO Association shall register this Constitution with the Registrar as per the stipulations outlined in the DAO Association Regulations.

19. Winding Up

(1) The DAO Association may be wound up voluntarily where:

    a.  the Governance Token Holders resolve by Governance Proposal that the DAO Association is to be wound up; and

    b.  one or more of the following conditions are met at the time of the passing of the Governance Proposal:

        i.  the expiry of the duration provided for in this Constitution;

        ii. where this Constitution provides for an expiration event, circumstance or condition when that event, circumstance or condition occurs;

        iii. the DAO Association is not able to appoint any further Council Members for any reason, including a shortage of appropriate candidates; or

        iv. the Council has passed a decision that the Purpose of the DAO Association has been achieved to its reasonable satisfaction.

(2) Where the DAO Association is to be wound up voluntarily, the Governance Token Holders shall appoint one or more auditors as liquidators by a Governance Proposal.

20. Amendment

    1.  Subject to the provisions of the DAO Association Regulations, the Governance Token Holders of a DAO Association may, by a Governance Proposal, propose to amend this Constitution.

    2.  If this Constitution of a DAO Association is amended, the rights and obligations of the Founding Members, Guarantee Members and/or the DAO Association that have arisen under the Constitution prior to the date of such amendment shall not be affected unless the amendment provides otherwise.

    3.  Notwithstanding anything in this Constitution, a Member of a DAO Association shall not be bound by an amendment made to the Constitution after the date on which they became a Member, insofar as the amendment:

        a.  creates or increases their Guarantee to the DAO Association or otherwise requires them to pay any amount to the DAO Association; or

        b.  amends their holding of or the rights, obligations or features of any Governance Token,

            unless they agree in writing, either before or after the amendment is made, to be bound by the amendment.

21. Governing Law

    This Constitution and all rights and obligations of the Founding Members, Council Members, Governance Token Holders and Officers of the DAO Association shall be governed and construed in accordance with the laws, rules, and regulations of the Authority and the applicable laws of Abu Dhabi Global Market (ADGM).

22. Notices

    A notice sent by the DAO Association to a Token Holder shall be deemed to have been given when it is posted to an Electronic forum, channel or platform to which the Token Holder had access at the time at which the notice was sent, such as discord or telegram group.

23. Miscellaneous

    1.  The DAO Association shall not perform any activities until it is registered in the DAO Association Register of the Authority.

    2.  Matters not provided for in this Constitution shall be subject to the provisions of the Regulations and any other applicable law in the Emirate of Ras Al Khaimah or the United Arab Emirates.

    3.  In the event this Constitution contains a provision that is contrary to or inconsistent with the Regulations, DAO Association Memorandum or any applicable law, provisions of the Regulations shall prevail.

    4.  In the event the decisions of the DAO Association Council conflict resolution of DAO Association Members, the resolution of DAO Association Members will prevail.
