If a DAO Association has issued or plans to issue tokens the following additional documents must be submitted:

-   A copy of the White Paper

-   A copy of the Tokenomics document (if not a part of White Paper)

-   A copy of a Legal Opinion on the White Paper and Tokenomics and the nature of the Virtual Asset (if any)

-   A copy of Cyber Security Audit

A Legal Opinion and a Cyber Security Audit must be issued by a reputable firm with the experience in the field.
