DAO CUSTOMER FAQs

General Provisions

1. What is the purpose of DAO Association?

To provide a legal framework for the establishment, governance, and operation of DAO Associations within the Ras Al Khaimah Digital Assets Oasis (RAK DAO).

2. What is the legislative authority for these regulations?

The Ras Al Khaimah Digital Assets Oasis Authority is empowered to issue and amend these regulations.

DAO Formation and Registration

3. What documents are required to incorporate a DAO?

Pls refer to the attached Document Checklist

4. Can a DAO operate before registration?

No, DAOs cannot conduct any business within the Zone until they are registered and issued a trade license.

5. What are the technical prerequisites for a DAO Association?

- It must be deployed on a Permissionless Distributed Ledger.

- Its software code must be open-source and publicly accessible.

6. What are the categories of DAO Associations?

DAOs may be designated as Startup DAO Associations or Alpha DAO Associations, depending on their purpose and operational stage.

8. What is the Constitution of a DAO?

It is the foundational document that defines governance, operations, decision-making processes, and member rights.

Governance and Management

9. What are the categories of membership in a DAO Association?

- Founding Members: Initial incorporators who provide guarantees for the DAO.

- Council Members: Executive and supervisory body responsible for DAO operations.

- Token Holders: Holders of Governance or Miscellaneous Tokens with specified rights.

- Registered Agents or Managers: Entities responsible for compliance and correspondence.

10. Can the Founding Members / Council Members be a Corporate Body?

Only Founding Members can be a corporate body.

11. What is the role of a Registered Agent or Manager?

To act as the representative of the DAO in the Zone, handling compliance, communication, and administrative functions.

12. Can Council Members delegate their responsibilities?

Yes, Council Members can appoint Alternate Council Members subject to approval by Governance Token Holders.

13. How is voting conducted in a DAO?

Decisions are typically made through Governance Token Holder votes, either at General Meetings or electronically.

14. What duties do Council Members have?

- Acting in the best interest of the DAO.

- Complying with UAE and RAK laws.

- Upholding the DAO’s Purpose as outlined in its Constitution.

15. How can a Council Member be removed?

Through a Governance Proposal approved by Governance Token Holders or as specified in the Constitution.

16. Can financial assistance be provided to Council Members?

Yes, but only with Governance Token Holder approval and compliance with UAE financial regulations.

Membership and Tokens

17. What are Governance Tokens?

Tokens that grant holders voting rights and other governance-related powers within the DAO.

18. What are Miscellaneous Tokens?

Tokens that provide access to services, track contributions, or confer other non-governance rights.

19. Can a DAO issue hybrid/multiple types of tokens?

Yes, provided the rights and obligations for each type are detailed in the Constitution.

20. What happens if a Token Holder gains 25% or more of voting rights?

They must declare their holdings to the Registrar within 15 days and may be subject to additional disclosures.

21. How can Token Holders dispose of their tokens?

The DAO’s Constitution must specify processes for voluntary and mandatory token disposals.

Financial and Compliance Requirements

22. What records must a DAO Association maintain?

- An Internal Register containing details of members, Council decisions, and governance records.

- Financial and operational records, including annual accounts.

- A register of Governance Tokens and their holders.

23. Who can access the DAO’s Internal Register?

Access is granted to the Registrar, Founding Members, Officers, and Token Holders. Non-members may access it only if permitted by the Constitution and upon payment of a fee.

24. What are the annual compliance requirements for DAOs?

- Submission of an Annual Return to the Registrar.

- Filing of audited financial statements.

- Renewal of trade licenses.

25. What are the penalties for non-compliance?

- Financial penalties imposed by the Registrar.

- Striking off the DAO from the Register.

- Legal action or liquidation proceedings.

26. What financial records must a DAO maintain?

The DAO must maintain comprehensive and accurate accounting records, which are subject to audit.

27. Who appoints the auditor for the DAO?

Governance Token Holders appoint the auditor, as specified in the Constitution.

28. Are DAOs required to submit financial statements?

Yes, annual accounts must be prepared and filed with the Registrar.

29. What happens if a DAO fails to comply with regulations?

It may face penalties, be struck off the Register, or face legal action by the Registrar.

Sub DAO Associations

30. What is a Sub DAO Association?

A subsidiary entity created by a DAO to carry out specific functions or projects.

31. How many Sub DAO can an Alpha DAO have?

A maximum of 10 (ten) Sub DAOs are permissible at an additional of [AMOUNT]per Sub DAO

32. What is required to register a Sub DAO Association?

An application must be submitted to the Registrar, detailing the Sub DAO’s purpose, management, and compliance mechanisms.

33. Who manages a Sub DAO Association?

A Sub DAO Manager, appointed by the parent DAO Association, oversees its operations.

34. What happens if a Sub DAO defaults?

The parent DAO is responsible for the Sub DAO’s liabilities unless explicitly limited by the Constitution or agreements.

35. Can assets be transferred between a DAO and its Sub DAO?

Yes, but such transfers must comply with the Constitution and applicable regulations.

Liquidation and Dissolution

36. What triggers the liquidation of a DAO?

- A decision by Governance Token Holders.

- Failure to comply with regulations.

- Court orders.

37. Who oversees the liquidation process?

A liquidator is appointed either by Governance Token Holders or the Court, depending on the circumstances.

38. What happens to DAO assets during liquidation?

Assets are distributed to settle liabilities, with any surplus managed per the DAO’s Constitution.

39. Can a dissolved DAO be restored?

Yes, subject to an application and approval by the Registrar or Court.

40. Can DAOs amalgamate?

Yes, two or more DAOs can merge into a single entity, subject to approval by the Registrar and compliance with applicable laws.

Legal and Regulatory Provisions

41. What are the Registrar’s powers?

- To approve or reject registrations.

- To enforce compliance with the regulations.

- To impose penalties for contraventions.

42. Can a DAO transfer its incorporation to another jurisdiction?

Yes, subject to approval by the Authority and compliance with the laws of the new jurisdiction.

43. How are disputes involving DAOs resolved?

Disputes may be referred to the Ras Al Khaimah Court or handled as per the DAO’s Constitution.

44. Can foreign DAOs incorporate in the Zone?

Yes, foreign DAOs can transfer incorporation to Ras Al Khaimah, provided they meet all regulatory requirements.

45. Can a DAO challenge the Registrar’s decisions?

No, the Registrar’s decisions are not subject to appeal or review unless otherwise specified.

46. How does a DAO comply with AML and KYC laws?

By verifying and maintaining detailed records of all members, token holders, and beneficial owners, as required by UAE federal laws.

Visa & Activities

47. Does the Startup DAO come with Visa?

No, Visa allocation and Visa issuance is not part of the package.

47. Does the Alpha DAO come with Visa?

Yes, 1(one) Visa allocation and Visa issuance is part of the package.

48. How many activities can a DAO have?

A DAO can have a maximum of 5 activities, with AED 2000 charged per activity per year from the third one. Also, the addition of activities is subject to approval.

49. Can a DAO be For-Profit?

No, a DAO registered with RAKDAO must be Not-for-Profit.

50. Can the treasury be in Crypto?

Yes, Treasury can comprise as a mix of both FIAT & Crypto.
