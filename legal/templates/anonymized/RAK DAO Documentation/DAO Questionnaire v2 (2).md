DAO QUESTIONNAIRE

Q1) Can you articulate the specific purpose of your proposed DAO and how it aligns with the allowed activities within the Zone?
Q2) How will your DAO's Constitution ensure this decentralization in practice, particularly concerning the roles and powers of Founding Members and the Council?

Q3) Will your DAO have a non-profit purpose? If so, how will you ensure compliance with the reinvestment and reallocation requirements outlined in the regulations?

Q4) Describe the different categories of membership within your DAO and the rights and obligations associated with each.

Q5) Will there be distinctions between Founding Members, Council Members, and Token Holders? How will you manage the onboarding of new members and ensure compliance with AML/CFT and KYC checks?

Q6) Explain the mechanisms for electing and removing Council Members and the term limits for their service.

Q7) Confirm that your DAO will be deployed on a permissionless Distributed Ledger and that the entire software code will be open-source and publicly accessible.

Q8) Explain how your DAO will manage its treasury, including processes for handling funds and assets

Q9) Provide a detailed breakdown of all Token classes, including Governance Tokens and Miscellaneous Tokens, and outline their utility or governance functions.

Q10) How will your DAO ensure compliance with all applicable UAE laws and regulations, including those related to AML/CFT, KYC, and securities?
